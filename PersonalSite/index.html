Console based Hexapawn

Game

Hexapawn is a simple game that was made to demonstrate machine learning. While I haven't implamented that, I have make a recreation of the game with an "AI" that plays against you. You can select the difficulty, and then play against it.

The rules are simple. On your turn, you can choose a pawn and move it either forward into an empty space, or diagonally forward replacing an enemy pawn. You win if you get a pawn to the end of the board or prevent your opponent from moving.

In order to play my version, type a 2 digit number representing the coords of the board of the piece you want to move. i.e. 11 = bottom left, 13 = bottom right, 31 = top left 33 = top right. If only one move with the piece is possible, it automatically moves. Otherwise you are asked if which move you want to do. Simply respond to the question. i.e. "Left or up" means you can move forward or diagonally left and forward.
